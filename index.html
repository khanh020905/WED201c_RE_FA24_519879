Mitsubishi Xforce 2024

After 2 years since bringing the Concept Xforce model to the Vietnam Motor Show 2022. On the morning of January 10, 2024, Mitsubishi Xforce 2024 was officially launched in the Vietnamese market with 4 versions, including Xforce GLX, Xforce Exceed, Xforce Premium and the most advanced version Xforce Utimate. Mitsubishi Xforce has received great attention from Vietnamese people because of its dynamic design and attractive price.

Based on the design concept of "Smooth and Solid", the All-New XFORCE possesses a strong, elegant and powerful appearance when operating in and out of the city. The front of the Mitsubishi Xforce makes a strong impression with the Dynamic Shield design language combined with the extremely large central radiator grille cluster, creating a modern and strong feeling.
